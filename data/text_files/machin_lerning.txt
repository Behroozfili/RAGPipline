Machine learning is a field of artificial intelligence that enables computers to learn from data and improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed. Instead of relying on fixed rules written by humans, machine learning systems identify patterns in data and use them to make predictions or decisions.

It is widely used in many real-world applications, such as recommendation systems, speech recognition, image classification, fraud detection, and autonomous vehicles. Machine learning models are typically trained using large datasets, where they learn relationships between inputs and outputs.

The main idea behind machine learning is to allow systems to adapt and improve as they are exposed to more data. This makes it a powerful tool for solving complex problems that are difficult to handle with traditional programming approaches.
